# Major Battles Before the Arena Campaign

## Red Ford Stand
A three-day defense where mixed-class squads delayed a numerically superior host.

## Siege of Hollow Meridian
Urban attrition battle proving that supply lines decide more wars than heroics.

## Embergate Countermarch
Coalition forces retook a mountain pass by rotating specialist teams every four hours.

## Night of Seven Flares
Assassin and Archer detachments disabled command beacons and broke enemy coordination.

These historical lessons inform current commander training inside the arena simulation framework.
