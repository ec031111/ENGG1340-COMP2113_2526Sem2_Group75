# Role Story: Archer

Archers descend from watchtower militias that defended trade roads.
They master distance judgment, patience, and timing under pressure.

Cultural code:
- See first, shoot second.
- Every arrow must have purpose.

Battle identity:
- Sustained ranged pressure.
- Finishing wounded targets and supporting frontline trades.

Campaign chronicles often describe Archers as the 'second heartbeat' of an army: less visible than the frontline, but essential to keeping battle tempo.
