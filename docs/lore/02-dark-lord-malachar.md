# Character Profile: Dark Lord Malachar

Malachar was once a siege mathematician in the old imperial academies.
After the Collapse, he turned to forbidden doctrines that merge blood-runes with logistical warfare.

Traits:
- Strategic patience: prefers long wars that drain morale and supply.
- Psychological pressure: spreads rumors before armies arrive.
- Adaptive command: rotates unit compositions to counter predictable defenses.

Narrative role in game:
- Explains increasing round pressure and enemy scaling.
- Represents the constant strategic threat behind each battle.

Symbolic meaning:
Malachar is less a single villain and more the embodiment of exhaustion.
Beating him means mastering planning under scarcity.
