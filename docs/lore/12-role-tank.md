# Role Story: Tank

Tanks are selected from fortress labor corps and veteran bodyguards.
Their training emphasizes pain tolerance, shield technique, and positional discipline.

Cultural code:
- The line breaks only with you.
- Endure first, strike second.

Battle identity:
- Damage absorption and anchor control.
- Protecting fragile allies from collapse.

In campaign records, Tank detachments are credited with the highest number of civilian convoy saves during retreat operations.
