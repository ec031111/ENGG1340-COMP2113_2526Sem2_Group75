# Factions and Cities of Aethoria

## Ironhold
Mountain citadel known for armor foundries and disciplined infantry.

## Luminreach
River city of scholars, artificers, and logistics planners.

## Thornwatch
Forest border-state with elite scouts and anti-ambush doctrine.

## Saltspire
Coastal trade republic financing coalition campaigns through convoys.

## Ashen Frontier
Ruined corridor where Malachar consolidated power.

Faction tension still exists, but coalition command keeps strategy aligned through rotating theater councils.
