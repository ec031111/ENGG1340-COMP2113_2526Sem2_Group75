# The Allied Forces

The Allied Forces are a coalition command structure, not a single nation.
Each city-state contributes officers, supplies, and specialist units.

Command principles:
1. Local autonomy in recruitment.
2. Shared tactical standards in battle.
3. Merit-based promotion by campaign endurance.

Why this matters to gameplay:
- Mixed-class squads are common and expected.
- Tactical flexibility is rewarded over rigid doctrine.
- Commanders must balance short-term wins with long-term survivability.

The player character is one of many regional commanders, but one of the few trusted with independent procurement authority.
That authority is represented by the shop phase economy decisions.
