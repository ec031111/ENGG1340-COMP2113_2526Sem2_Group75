# Magic, Relics, and Battlefield Technology

Aethoria blends practical engineering with ritual magic.
Most armies rely on standard steel, but relics can change tactical assumptions.

Relic classes:
- Ward Relics: reduce incoming spell volatility in a zone.
- Pulse Relics: amplify morale and reaction speed in nearby troops.
- Echo Relics: preserve tactical memory from prior campaigns.

Military doctrine warns against relic overreliance.
Relics fail unpredictably under corruption storms, so commanders are trained to win with fundamentals first.

This mirrors game design: strong tactical foundations outperform luck-dependent spikes over long runs.
