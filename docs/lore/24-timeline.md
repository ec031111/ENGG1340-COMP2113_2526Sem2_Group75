# Timeline: From Collapse to Current War

- Year 0: Collapse of Crowns begins.
- Year 2: Famine migration and militia uprisings spread.
- Year 4: Black Concord signed by surviving city-states.
- Year 6: First sightings of Malachar's ash-banner legions.
- Year 7: Ironhold supply tunnels sabotaged.
- Year 8: Red Ford Stand buys coalition time.
- Year 9: Luminreach formalizes mixed-class command manuals.
- Year 10: Seventh Defensive Campaign declared.
- Present: Arena commanders tested under escalating round pressure.

The timeline is intentionally compact so new players can understand setting context quickly.
