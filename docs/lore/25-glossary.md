# Glossary of Setting Terms

## Aethoria
The main continent where coalition and frontier wars take place.

## Black Concord
The survival alliance between five city-states.

## Ashen Frontier
Region under Malachar's strategic influence.

## Campaign Round
A command phase representing one cycle of recruitment, formation, and battle.

## Coalition Theater Council
Rotating command committee coordinating city-state priorities.

## Relic Storm
Unstable magical weather that can disrupt artifacts and communications.

## Attrition Doctrine
War strategy focused on exhausting enemy resources over time.

## Independent Procurement Authority
Commander privilege to recruit and allocate units directly.
