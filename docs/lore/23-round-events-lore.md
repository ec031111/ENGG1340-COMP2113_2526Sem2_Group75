# Lore Interpretation of Random Events

Random events are represented as theater-level incidents outside direct commander control.

Examples:
- Gold Rush: emergency treasury release from coalition reserve vaults.
- Shop Sale: temporary wartime subsidy from local quartermasters.
- Divine Blessing: medical caravans reaching frontline camps in time.
- Lucky Find: recovered caches from abandoned outposts.
- Tax Collector: emergency levy imposed by city councils.
- Enemy Buff: Malachar receives reinforcement from frontier cults.

This framing keeps events narratively coherent while preserving uncertainty in campaign planning.
