# Role Story: Assassin

Assassins are recruited from scouts, tunnel-runners, and frontier hunters.
They specialize in infiltration, target isolation, and disruption.

Cultural code:
- Silence is part of strategy.
- One precise strike is worth ten reckless charges.

Battle identity:
- Backline disruption.
- High-risk elimination of priority threats.

In lore, Assassin companies are politically controversial but militarily indispensable.
They are deployed when conventional lines cannot break enemy command rhythm.
