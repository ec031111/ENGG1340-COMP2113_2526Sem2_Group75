# Role Story: Warrior

Warriors come from shield-tribes and line infantry schools where discipline is sacred.
They are trained to hold chokepoints and punish overextended enemies.

Cultural code:
- Never abandon formation without order.
- Protect ranged allies first, pursue glory second.

Battle identity:
- Reliable frontline pressure.
- Momentum through sustained engagement.

In campaign lore, Warrior captains are usually the first to volunteer for evacuation-cover operations.
Their battlefield presence symbolizes stability when panic spreads.
