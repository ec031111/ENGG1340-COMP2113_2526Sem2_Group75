# Role Story: Mage

Mages are graduates of ember academies and storm observatories.
They study battlefield geometry, line-of-sight, and controlled burst casting.

Cultural code:
- Precision is mercy.
- Waste no spell where steel is enough.

Battle identity:
- Area pressure and tempo swings.
- Punishing clustered enemies.

In narrative terms, Mages are strategic equalizers.
A single well-timed cast can compensate for inferior numbers.
