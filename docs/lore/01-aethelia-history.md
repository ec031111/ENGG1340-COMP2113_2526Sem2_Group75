# The History of Aethoria

## 1. Age of Banners
Five dynasties ruled separate regions and competed through ritualized wars.
Military guilds developed formal unit classes that later became modern battlefield roles.

## 2. The Collapse of Crowns
A succession crisis triggered famine, plague, and civil war.
Royal armies disbanded, and independent companies took over local defense.

## 3. The Black Concord
Survivors formed a fragile alliance to rebuild trade roads and grain routes.
The alliance created the first joint command charter for mixed-class armies.

## 4. Rise of Malachar
A frontier warlock-lord unified exiled warbands under a doctrine of attrition.
His armies advanced by exhausting cities instead of conquering them quickly.

## 5. Seventh Defensive Campaign
The current era: commanders are evaluated by how many rounds they can hold.
This is the direct narrative frame of Auto-Battler Arena.
